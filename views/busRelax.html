Bus Relax

  • FIELD: UX Research|UX Design | Interactive Design
  • DATE: 2017.9 | one week
  • Process: Research | Analysis | Idea Generation | Prototyping

Bus Relax is an application on tracking bus and enjoying the journey on s bus. Different from the most existing bus application, I added the function of checking the condition of coming buses and get off alarm.

This is a project of User Centered Design course in Coursera.

Interview

In order to understand user's real need, I did a user interview to learn more about her exprience on transportation.

View Interview Report

What I have learned from my research

  • People often want to relax on transportation
  • There is little information about situation on transportation
