About

I live in Taipei and have a Bachelor's degree in Geosciences from National Taiwan University. I have great passions to explore the world, however, instead of figuring out what happened inside the planet Earth( That’s exactly what Geosciences students do), I am more interested in people and countries above the Earth surface.

I spent a whole year in Japan as an exchange student, major in Japanese. During this period, I discovered the world of user experience research. I became hungry for great user-centered design and problem-solving.  I taught myself user search and user design by courses on Coursera. I love people and technology, especially technology with amazing usability, which is why I dive into the world of UX.

Languages interest me a lot: I speak Chinese natively and I love Japanese. Japanese in the key to open the gate to entertainment for me. I've been learning English for a handful of years and did half a year of French at university. The biggest interest I gain from languages is knowing the live and idea of people who use those languages.

On weekends, you will find me watching the newest Japanese TV programs, and sometimes translating the subtitle at the same time. I also like swimming and jogging.
