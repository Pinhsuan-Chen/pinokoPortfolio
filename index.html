About

Front-end engineer providing a seamless bridge between UI/UX design and the back-end. Experience with developing single-page websites in Vue.js. and UX design. Specialties include responsive web design and JavaScript. Actively seeking for opportunity to utilize problem solving skills and attention to detail to further develop my abilities in the field of computer Science.

I live in Taipei and have a Bachelor's degree in Geosciences from National Taiwan University. I have great passions to explore the world, however, instead of figuring out what happened inside the planet Earth( That’s exactly what Geosciences students do), I am more interested in people and countries above the Earth surface.

I spent a whole year in Japan as an exchange student, major in Japanese. During this period, I discovered the world of user experience research. I became hungry for great user-centered design and problem-solving.  I taught myself UX design and front-end programming. I love people and technology, especially technology with amazing usability.

Languages interest me a lot: I speak Chinese natively and I love Japanese. Japanese in the key to open the gate of Japanese culture for me. I've been learning English for a handful of years and did half a year of French at university. The biggest interest I gain from languages is knowing the lives and idea of people from different places around the world.

On weekends, you will find me learning front-end news or technique, watching the newest Japanese TV programs, and sometimes translating the subtitle at the same time. I also like swimming and jogging.
